Hakol Tov: Looking back at mistakes I made, so you won't have to repeat them

Don't get divorced. And while you're at it -
Don't get married. The concept of "'till death do us part" committed suicide. Everything ends, and so will your relationship. When that withers, you're back to square one, usually with far less than when you entered. People are not monogamists. They may be very good at pretending, but eventually they'll opt for replacing their partners.
